# First 10 Prospects — Outreach Playbook

The fastest path from "live site" to "first paying client" is 10 in-person visits
in 7 days. This is the playbook.

## The math

- 10 visits → 4–6 real conversations → 1–2 signed clients
- At $997/mo, that's **$1,000–$2,000/mo recurring** within 2 weeks
- Time cost: 2–3 hours/day for one week

## Who to target

**Local trade businesses with bad websites.** Not the worst businesses — the worst
businesses don't care. You want businesses doing $500K–$3M/yr with outdated
sites that are visibly costing them leads.

**Easy signals they need you:**
- Site loads in 4+ seconds
- No mobile responsiveness (text tiny, buttons overlap)
- No chat / no way to get a quote online
- Last blog post is from 2019
- No Google reviews from past 6 months
- Owner doesn't have a Google Business Profile photo

## How to find them (45 min)

1. Google `[your trade] + [your city]` — plumber, electrician, HVAC, roofer
2. Open the top 10 results
3. Note which have bad sites (most will)
4. Get the owner's name from the contact page or Google Business Profile
5. Get a phone number

**For your first week, focus on PLUMBERS** — biggest market, biggest spend.

## The visit (5–10 min each)

Walk in. Bring a tablet (phone is fine) showing the live demo on ventrix-agency.com.

### The opener (literally what to say)

> "Hey, I'm [your first name] with Ventrix — I help local [trade] companies
> get a website that actually books jobs instead of just sitting there.
> Got 3 minutes? I want to show you something on my tablet."

If they say yes:

> "Look — this is a live demo of an AI receptionist. Watch — it answers
> questions, captures leads, books appointments. Imagine this on YOUR
> website, working for you at 2am when someone's sink is leaking."

Type a quick message in the demo on your tablet. Let them see the response.

> "This is what your customers would see. They'd type in 'my hot water's out'
> or 'I have a leak under the sink' and get an immediate, helpful response
> that captures their info and pings you overnight.
>
> Right now, if someone calls you at 11pm and you don't answer, they call
> the next plumber on Google. That's a job you already lost.
>
> We charge $[997]/month for the website plus the AI agent. No setup fee.
> Month to month. Cancel any time.
>
> Want me to come back Tuesday with a proposal?"

### If they push back on price

> "What does a job pay you on average? 800? 1500? 3000?
> So one extra job a month covers the fee. We typically land 3–5x that in
> the first 90 days, but even at the worst case — one job — it pays for itself."

### If they want to "think about it"

> "Totally. I'll send you a one-pager with what we'd build and what it costs.
> What's the best email?"

Then send the one-pager (see `proposal-one-pager.md`).

## Common objections

**"I'm too busy for this right now."**
> "I get it — that's exactly why you'd want this. The agent handles the
> leads you don't have time for. 10 minutes now saves 20 hours later."

**"I have a guy who does my website."**
> "Great — is he getting you leads? I'm not asking you to fire him. I'm asking
> if you want MORE leads. We can work alongside him or take it over. Either way."

**"I tried AI stuff before, didn't work."**
> "What happened? [Listen.] Yeah, that's why we tune it specifically to YOUR
> business. Want to see the demo before deciding?"

**"Send me an email."**
> "Sure — what's the best address? [Get it.] I'll send the one-pager tonight."

## After the visit

Same day, send the email (template in `cold-email.md`). Don't wait.

If they said "come back Tuesday" — put it in your calendar with their business
name as the title.

## What NOT to do

- Don't walk in with a printed brochure — outdated energy
- Don't lead with technology — lead with their problem (missed jobs)
- Don't badmouth their current site — they may have built it themselves
- Don't ask "is the owner in?" — ask for them by name
- Don't argue. If they're not interested, smile, thank them, leave. There are 9 more.

## What to wear

Polo + clean jeans + clean shoes. Not a suit (you're not a banker). Not a t-shirt
(you're not a kid). Dress one step above their field techs.

## What to bring

- Tablet or phone, charged
- Live demo on ventrix-agency.com open in browser
- 5 business cards (with your name, ventrixagency.com, phone)
- A printed one-pager (3 copies, just in case)
