# Cold Email — Never Met Them

Use this for prospects you HAVEN'T visited in person. Lower conversion
(2–5%) but higher volume. Best for filling your pipeline.

## How to find them

Google "[trade] + [city]" — look at sites ranked 1–10. If their site is
slow, dated, or has no chat, that's a prospect.

## Template

Subject: Your [trade] website is losing you jobs

Hi [First Name],

Quick observation — [Business Name]'s website takes [X] seconds to load
and has no way for visitors to get a quote after hours.

I'm a local dev — I built a free AI receptionist demo that handles
after-hours leads automatically: https://ventrix-agency.com/demo

It's trained specifically for [trade] businesses. Visitors type "my
[problem]" and it responds like a real person, captures the lead, and
emails you immediately.

If you'd like a free 30-minute walkthrough where I show you exactly
what I'd build for [Business Name] and what it would cost, just reply
"audit" and I'll send a video.

— [Benny]
Ventrix Agency
[benny@ventrixagency.com]

## Send limits

- Send 10–20 per day
- Personalize the subject line with their city or business name when possible
- Don't send on Mondays (inbox is full) or Fridays (everyone's checked out)
- Best days: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday morning (8–10am)
